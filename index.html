London Joseph

I am an undergraduate student attending Portland State University majoring in computer science. I’m a non-traditional student whose interest in computers and programming was piqued later in life than your average CS major.

Before shifting careers I had spent over 10 years in the culinary space working as a cook in the Portland area. Though the two fields are worlds apart I value the time spent in my former field and have found that the interpersonal skills developed in the restaurant industry have translated well to the STEM environment. The ability to work in a team, think critically, solve problems and manage both time and stress have proven to be great assets as I’ve transitioned into the tech industry.

Through my coursework at Portland State, I have developed the technical skillset required of a successful and well-rounded developer. I have gained fluency in high-level languages such as Java, C++, and Python as well as proficiency in data structures, algorithms, and problem analysis. I’ve also discovered an interest in data and data analysis and have begun to lean more heavily into data science, shifting my curriculum focus to artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data visualization techniques. Post-graduation, my goal is to find a role working with and creating intelligent systems.
